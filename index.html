Signature Fine Wine, Spirits & Craft Beer is Beaumont's local destination for premium selections and everyday favourites — all under one roof.

We're proud to serve the Beaumont community with a carefully curated inventory that spans the full spectrum of beverages. Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Signature Fine Wine located in Beaumont?

We are located at 4904 30 Ave, Beaumont, AB, conveniently serving Beaumont and surrounding communities. You can call us at (780) 737-1227 or get directions via Google Maps.

What are your hours?

Our current store hours are:

  • Monday – Thursday & Sunday: 10:00 a.m. – 11:00 p.m.
  • Friday – Saturday: 10:00 a.m. – 12:00 a.m.
Do you carry local Alberta craft beer?

Yes! We carry a wide range of Alberta and Canadian craft beers, including selections from Alley Kat, Blindman, Cabin, Banded Peak, Sea Change, Troubled Monk, Collective Arts, and more. Selection may vary — please contact the store for current availability.

Can I order liquor in bulk for a wedding or event?

Absolutely. We specialize in event and wedding liquor orders for Beaumont and surrounding area. Fill out our Event & Wedding Quote form and our team will contact you with a personalized quote. We handle weddings, corporate events, private parties, and community functions.

Do you have a wine room?

Yes! Our wine room is a boutique-style section of the store designed for customers looking for premium bottles, gifting options, and curated wine selections. Our knowledgeable team is always happy to help you find the right bottle.

Do you carry non-alcoholic beverages?

Yes, we carry non-alcoholic wines, beers, and beverages for those who prefer alcohol-free options. Selection may vary — please contact the store for current availability.

What spirits do you carry?

We carry a full range of spirits including whisky, Scotch, bourbon, tequila, mezcal, vodka, rum, gin, and liqueurs. Both premium and everyday price points are available. Selection may vary — please contact the store for current availability.
